Instant Intelligence With Real-Time Dashboards

Scattered data sources and out-of-date reports make it difficult to make smart, informed decisions. Firms managing a portfolio of investments need to access not only internal metrics, but metrics for each of their investment companies. Connected Intel is a perfect solution for investors to have a clear perspective on the performance of their firm as a whole, as well as each investment.

Data isn’t just numbers—it should tell a story. By uniting all of your data sources, you can get the full story of your business, and Connected Intel's interface allows you to present your story with beautiful, clear visualizations that are easy to analyze and interpret.

If you are VC or Private Equity firm and want to give the gift of success to your portfolio companies, require such a data dashboard before investing. Our data dashboard has the following benefits:


All investment professionals within the fund operate on the same metrics and insights.




Increase transparency and accountability.




Management saves valuable hours from preparing for traditional board reporting.



Investment decisions become simpler for follow-on funds or cap structure changes based on real time performance.



Investors have summary dashboards with logins for analyst, associates and partners based on what they want to see .


Funds can better report on performance to their institutional investors by aggregating portfolio company data into an overall fund metric.
