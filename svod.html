Streaming Video On Demand 


For business' offering of streaming video on-demand (SVOD) services. It is important to see the full picture across major streaming platforms such as Roku, iOS, and the web. Connected Intelligence provides a tool that can piece apart their volumes of data to understand their subscribers: who they are, why and when the make a purchase and why they stop subscriptions.

GET STARTED

Engagement   

Know the number of subscribers by engagement (for example, X subscribers are dormant, Y subscribers are from occasional viewers, etc.). 

Paid Subscriptions

Visual metrics of owned and operated paid subscribers relative to the calendar date exactly one year prior and YOY varience.

Retention & Survival

Visualizaiton detailing retention. This shows, for all the subscriotions in a given month, how many subscriptions remain on 1 month, 2 months, and so on up to 12 months. 

Cancellers

Insightful view of average monthly churn. This visual tells, on average, how many people unsubscribe from a campaign/product every month.
