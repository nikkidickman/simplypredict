What We Do

At Predict we work with C-Suite executives and Investors transform their businesses. We bring together the right people, tools, and data to transform your business, delivering strategic data solutions across the enterprise. Data should be actionable at all levels of the organization and visualized for leadership to drive strategy. Predict works closely with client teams, often in short to mid-term roles, to personally develop roadmaps, sales funnels, targeted campaigns, go-to-market programs, brandings, product definitions, partnerships, and M&A – for market leaders, under-performing units, and new ventures.

Additionally, we spend significant time investigating related data and digital companies and markets, fleshing out opportunities for institutional investments. We bring deep industry knowledge, curiosity, and passion to help clients evolve from being data-deficient to data-driven.

Data Due Diligence

A critical step in every deal process is due diligence. Both buyers and sellers want to make sure the “marriage” is a good fit. Sellers want to ensure they are getting a good value for shareholders, and buyers want to ensure a strong return on investment. When an organization’s data is properly managed – both internal data as well as the data from suppliers, partners, and customers – it simplifies due diligence.

Chris Bryant
Managing Partner
CONSULTING SERVICES

Chris has more than 20 years of digital marketing experience, having founded one of the first big dot com agencies in 1995. T3 Media was born and quickly grew into the leading provider of interactive marketing services for Broadway’s biggest shows, including Phantom of the Opera, Les Misérables, Cats and many others. 

By 1997, T3 Media became the Interactive Agency of Record for major Fortune 500 brands like Microsoft, Deutsche Bank and American Express. In just five years, Chris successfully built the company into a national presence, and ultimately sold the company in 1999. Chris was a finalist for the Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year in 1999.

Since 2003 Chris has been involved as a partner, board member or adviser to 10 highly varied businesses including Elizabeth Street Gallery, importing 18th and 19th Century European carved stone statuary, garden ornaments and architectural elements; Rhino Mobile, a leading mobile advertising network; and the Entrepreneurs’ Organization. A member for 15+ years, he has served on the New York City chapter board including three years as President from 2005-2016. He also served on EO’s Global Board from 2011-2014, overseeing the organization's 116 chapters in 42 countries.

Jeff Marcus
Managing Partner
TECHNOLOGY & DATA

Jeff elevates companies to the next stage of innovation with IT services such as technology, leadership, strategy and development.

Previously, Jeff served as CTO of 24/7 Real Media (now Xaxis), leading up to the sale to WPP.
